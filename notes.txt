Embeddings are vectors that represent the meaning of text as numbers.
ChromaDB is a vector database that stores embeddings and allows semantic search.
Gemini is Google's large language model that can generate text responses.
RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation which combines search with AI generation.
Tokenization is the process of splitting text into smaller units called tokens.
LangGraph is a framework for building multi-agent systems using graph structures.
Fine-tuning means training a model on your own data for a specific task.
Context window is the maximum amount of text a model can process at once.
Agents are AI systems that can reason, use tools, and take actions autonomously.
Vector similarity measures how close two embeddings are in meaning.
